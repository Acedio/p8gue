# Boggarts & Billiards

Boggarts & Billiards is a small roguelike made for the 2026 7DRL jam. You play as Bop, a goblin on a quest with his trusty companion, Ball. Throw Ball around to defend yourself while you descend through the three floors of the dungeon and attain your delicious reward :)

## Controls

Arrow Keys - Move

Press Z - Pick up Ball.

(While holding Ball) Press and hold Z - Aim with arrow keys

(While holding Ball) Release Z - Throw Ball in the aimed direction
